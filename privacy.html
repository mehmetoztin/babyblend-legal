Privacy Policy

Application: BabyBlend AI
Effective Date: 2025-12-13

This Privacy Policy explains how BabyBlend AI (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Photos and Image Processing

You may upload photos for AI processing. Photos are used only to generate results and are not stored permanently.

  • Photos are transmitted securely for processing.
  • Photos are deleted automatically after generation is completed.
  • Photos are not used to train AI models.

2. Generated Images

Generated images may be temporarily stored to deliver results and are removed after a short retention period.

3. Subscription and Purchase Data

Subscription and purchase status is handled through Apple’s App Store.

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription entitlements and verify premium access. RevenueCat processes limited technical identifiers related to purchases but does not receive uploaded photos.

4. Analytics and Technical Data

We may collect anonymous usage data and crash reports through services such as Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics. This data does not include uploaded photos.

5. Data Retention

  • Uploaded photos: deleted after processing.
  • Generated images: temporary retention only.
  • Analytics data: retained according to third-party policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user data. However, no system is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of changes.
