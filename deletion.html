Data Deletion Instructions

Application: BabyBlendAI
Effective date: 2025-12-27

BabyBlendAI allows users to request deletion of their account-related data.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us at babyblendai@hotmail.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include your request and, if applicable, the email address used in the app (or any identifier you used).

What will be deleted

  • Account-related records (if any)
  • Server-side logs tied to your identifier (where applicable and legally permitted)
